# annotaR 0.1.0

* Initial release of `annotaR`.
* Core annotation functions:
    * `annotaR()` to initialize the pipeline.
    * `add_go_terms()` for functional enrichment from g:Profiler.
    * `add_disease_links()` for disease associations from OpenTargets.
    * `add_drug_links()` for known drug associations from OpenTargets.
* Visualization:
    * `plot_enrichment_dotplot()` for creating publication-ready dot plots of enrichment results.
* Full suite of unit tests for core functions.
* Includes a "getting started" vignette (`annotaR-workflow`).
